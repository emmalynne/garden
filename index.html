Welcome!

The purpose of the Karen Nash Memorial Butterfly Garden, Incorporated (aka KNMBG) is to develop and maintain a schoolyard wildlife habitat garden on the grounds of Memorial School located at 300 West Stewart St., Washington, New Jersey for the purpose of education, community service, and public use.

The KNMBG Inc. has been designated as a 501-c- 3 educational, not-for-profit organization.

Our website is new and still under development. If you have any questions or concerns about something that isn't on the website, please don't hesitate to contact us. We would love to hear from you!

About KNMBG

The Karen Nash Memorial Butterfly Garden is a nonprofit organization made up of community volunteers, parents and teachers. We work with the students of Memorial School in our youth-driven community garden located on the school grounds, in the heart of Warren County, NJ.
